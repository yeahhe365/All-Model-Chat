# Code of Conduct

AMC WebUI welcomes constructive participation from users and contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient when discussing bugs, support requests, and design trade-offs.
- Focus feedback on the work, reproduction details, and user impact.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence when a report is unclear.
- Keep discussions on topic and useful for maintainers and future readers.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Posting private information, credentials, tokens, or other sensitive data.
- Repeated off-topic comments or low-effort noise after maintainers ask for clarification.
- Public disclosure of security vulnerabilities before maintainers have a chance to respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or block participants when behavior harms the project community.
